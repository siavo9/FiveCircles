📖 How to Play

Four Circles is a colorful guessing game! A secret code of 4 colors is hidden. You have 10 tries to guess the code by picking colors and getting clues after each guess.

  • 🎯 Goal: Crack the secret 4-color code in 10 guesses or fewer.
  • 🎨 Pick: Tap colors from the palette to build your guess.
  • ✅ Green dot: right color, right spot.
  • 🟡 Yellow dot: right color, wrong spot.
  • ⚪ Gray dot: color is not in the code.
