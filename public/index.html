Four Circles — The Free Color Code Puzzle Game

Four Circles is a free online puzzle game that challenges your brain every time you play. A secret sequence of 4 colors is hidden — your job is to crack the code in 10 guesses using pure logic and memory. After every guess you get instant feedback: which colors are in the right spot, which exist in the code but are misplaced, and which aren't there at all. It's a thinking game that's easy to learn but deeply satisfying to master. Inspired by the classic Mastermind board game, Four Circles brings the beloved code-breaking experience to your browser for free.

Like Wordle, But With Colors

If you love Wordle, you'll feel right at home. Four Circles follows the same satisfying loop — make a guess, read the clues, refine your logic, and solve the puzzle. Instead of letters and words, you're reasoning about colors. Choose from 6 vibrant colors — Red, Blue, Yellow, Green, Orange, and Purple — each with their own personality. Play in Easy mode (no repeated colors) or Hard mode (repeats allowed) for an extra challenge. Fans of Colorle, Colorfle, and other color guessing games will love Four Circles.

How Four Circles Compares to Other Puzzle Games

Four Circles sits in the sweet spot between classic code-breaking games like Mastermind and modern daily puzzle games like Wordle. While Wordle tests your vocabulary, Four Circles tests your logical reasoning and color pattern recognition. It's simpler to pick up than Sudoku, more colorful than crosswords, and more strategic than most casual puzzle games. Whether you call it a color code game, a logic puzzle, or a brain teaser — it's endlessly replayable.

📖 How to Play

🎯 The Goal

A secret 4-color code has been hidden. Guess it within 10 tries to win!

🎨 The Colors

💡 How It Works

  • Click colors from the palette to build your 4-color guess.
  • Click a filled slot to remove that color.
  • Hit Submit Guess to lock it in.
  • After each guess you get feedback (see below).

📊 Reading the Feedback

Right spot — correct color AND correct position
Wrong spot — correct color but wrong position
Not in code — this color isn't in the secret at all

⚙️ Easy vs Hard Mode

  • 😌 Easy: The secret code has no repeated colors — each color appears at most once.
  • 🔥 Hard: Colors can repeat in the secret code!
